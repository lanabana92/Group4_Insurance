Compulsory civil liability insurance of motor vehicle owners is a type of compulsory insurance prescribed by the State. All motor vehicle owners, individuals, organizations (including foreigners) owning motor vehicles in Vietnam must participate in order to protect and ensure the interests of third parties if they are unfortunately injured. and property caused by traffic accidents.

Motor vehicle owners participating in traffic and operating in the territory of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam.
  • The insurance validity begins and ends according to the period stated in the Certificate of Insurance.
  • For two-wheeled, three-wheeled motorcycles, mopeds (including electric scooters) and vehicles of similar structure as prescribed by the Road Traffic Law, the minimum insurance period is 1 year and maximum of 3 years .
  • During the valid period indicated on the Certificate of Insurance , if there is a transfer of motor vehicle ownership, all insurance benefits related to the civil liability of the old motor vehicle owner will still be valid for the owner. new motor vehicle.
  • The time limit for claiming compensation is 1 year from the date of the accident, except in case of delay due to objective and force majeure causes as prescribed by law.
  • Within 5 working days from the date of the accident (except in case of force majeure), the insurance buyer and the insured must send the accident notice in writing or via electronic form to the insurance company. insurance industry.
  • The time limit for compensation payment of the insurer is 15 days from the date of receipt of a complete and valid insurance claim dossier and no later than 30 days from the date of receipt of a complete insurance claim dossier. valid insurance in the event that the verification of records is required.
  • In case of refusal to indemnify, the insurer must notify in writing the insurance buyer and the insured the reason for the refusal within 30 days from the date of receipt of a complete claim dossier. valid insurance claim.
  • The dossier includes the documents specified in Article 15 of Decree No. 03/2021/ND-CP issued on January 15, 2021 by the Government on civil liability insurance of motor vehicle owners.
